A game made from scratch by a team of 9 students using Scrum.

The game is about playing as a clumsy football player and picking up power-ups, but be careful some of them are rigged against you! Kick your way to victory!

Coded in C# and created in Unity.

[Try it out!](https://noahpe0.wixsite.com/klantboll/game)

[Github](https://github.com/filhed97/Klantboll?tab=readme-ov-file)

## Game Development Responsibilities
- Kicking functionality
- Prototype movement
- Ragdoll functionality
- Animation switching
- Acting Product Owner
